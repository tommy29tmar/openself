## 1. EU Institutional Grants & Programs

### 1.1 EIC Accelerator (European Innovation Council)

- **Managing entity**: European Innovation Council (EIC), European Commission
- **Type**: Blended finance (grant + equity), grant-only, or equity-only
- **Amount**:
  - Grant: up to EUR 2.5M (TRL 6-8 activities)
  - Equity/quasi-equity: EUR 0.5M - 10M (market deployment/scale-up)
  - Blended: EUR 2.5M grant + up to EUR 10M equity
- **2026 Budget**: EUR 634M total (EUR 414M Open + EUR 220M Challenges)
- **Key requirements**:
  - SMEs, startups, or small mid-caps (up to 499 employees)
  - Breakthrough innovation with high risk / market-creating potential
  - One application at a time per entity
  - Technology areas include AI explicitly
- **Application process**: Two-stage
  - Stage 1: Short application (written proposal, pitch deck, 3-min video) - submit anytime, 4-6 weeks processing
  - Stage 2: Full proposal (20-page form, implementation plan, financials, FTO analysis) at fixed cut-off dates
- **2026 Cut-off dates (Stage 2)**: January 7, March 4, May 6, July 8, September 2, November 4
- **Interview sessions**: After every second batch
- **URL**: https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-accelerator_en
- **Notes**: Major change in 2026 - moved from 2 cut-offs to 6 batching dates. Faster processing. AI is explicitly listed as a technology area.

### 1.2 Horizon Europe - Cluster 4 (Digital, Industry and Space)

- **Managing entity**: European Commission / HaDEA (Health and Digital Executive Agency)
- **Type**: Collaborative R&D grants (typically 70-100% funding rate for SMEs)
- **Amount**: Varies by topic
  - HORIZON-CL4-2026-01: EUR 319M (15 topics, single stage)
  - HORIZON-CL4-2026-02: EUR 98M (3 topics, two-stage)
  - HORIZON-RAISE-2026-01: EUR 27.8M (2 topics)
  - HORIZON-CID-2026-01: EUR 125M (1 topic)
- **Key requirements**:
  - Consortium of at least 3 entities from 3 different EU/associated countries
  - AI-relevant topics include "Apply AI" and GenAI4EU initiatives
  - Topics range from robotics to quantum sensors
- **Application info**: Published on EU Funding & Tenders Portal
- **URL**: https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/
- **Notes**: Requires consortium (not solo applicants). Better suited for collaborative R&D projects.

### 1.3 Open Horizons - Women-Led Deeptech Startups

- **Managing entity**: Open Horizons (EU-funded project)
- **Type**: Equity-free grant
- **Amount**: Up to EUR 55,000
- **Key requirements**: Women-led deeptech and digital startups
- **Application info**: Open Call 3 opens March 2026
- **URL**: https://www.openhorizonsproject.eu/open-calls/
- **Notes**: Not applicable unless there is a female co-founder/leader.

### 1.4 Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL)

- **Managing entity**: European Commission / HaDEA
- **Type**: Grants (typically 50% co-funding, up to 75% for specific actions)
- **Amount**: Varies by call (typically EUR 1-5M per project)
- **Key 2026 AI calls**:
  - DIGITAL-2026-AI-09: AI Continent (multiple sub-topics)
  - DIGITAL-2026-AI-09-GENAI-PA: Deployment of Generative AI in public administrations
  - Virtual worlds / extended reality testbeds
  - AI-based medical imaging solutions
- **Key requirements**:
  - Usually requires consortium (2+ entities from different EU countries)
  - Focus on deployment rather than research
  - Digital skills, AI deployment, cybersecurity are priority areas
- **Recent deadlines**: 3 March 2026 for the November 2025 batch of calls
- **URL**: https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/activities/get-funding-digital
- **Notes**: GenAI4EU initiative specifically targets "Generative AI made in Europe." Deployment-focused, so less suited for early-stage startups building their own product.

### 1.6 Eurostars (Eureka Network)

- **Managing entity**: Eureka Network, co-funded by Horizon Europe
- **Type**: Collaborative R&D grant
- **Amount**: EUR 300-500K per partner (varies by national funding body)
- **Key requirements**:

  - Consortium: at least 2 independent entities from at least 2 Eurostars countries (at least 1 EU/associated)
  - SME budgets >= 50% of total project costs
  - No single participant/country > 70% of budget

### 1.7 InvestEU / EIF (European Investment Fund)

- **Managing entity**: European Investment Fund (EIF), part of EIB Group
- **Type**: Indirect VC investment (EIF invests in VC funds, not directly in startups)
- **How it works**: EIF backs VC funds that then invest in startups. In 2025, EIF invested EUR 687M in active funds in Italy (highest ever).
- **Key EIF-backed Italian funds**:
  - DaVinci Growth Capital Fund: EUR 80M target (EUR 20M from EIF), growth-stage Italian companies
  - CDP Venture Capital funds (see Italian section below)
  - Indico Capital Partners: EUR 125M fund for Portugal/Spain/Italy tech
- **How to access**: Apply to EIF-backed VC funds (not directly to EIF)
- **URL**: https://www.eif.org/
- **Notes**: Not a direct application. OpenSelf would benefit indirectly by pitching to EIF-backed Italian VC funds.

## 2. Italian National Programs

### 2.1 Smart&Start Italia (Invitalia / MIMIT)

- **Managing entity**: Invitalia, on behalf of MIMIT (Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy)
- **Type**: Zero-interest loan (+ grant component for Southern Italy)
- **Amount**: EUR 100,000 - 1,500,000 at 0% interest, no collateral required
  - Covers up to 80% of eligible expenses (90% for women-led or youth startups)
  - Southern Italy: additional grant component (fondo perduto)
- **Key requirements**:
  - Must be a "startup innovativa" registered in the special section of the Business Register
  - Less than 60 months since incorporation
  - Registered office and operations in Italy
- **Application**: Always open (sportello). No deadlines, evaluated chronologically.
- **New platform**: Since 3 November 2025, new online application platform for streamlined submissions
- **URL**: https://www.invitalia.it/en/incentives-and-tools/smartstart-italia
- **Eligible expenses**: Capital goods, services, personnel costs, operating costs
- **Notes**: THE most relevant Italian national program for OpenSelf. No deadlines, always open. Zero interest, no collateral. Very strong fit for an AI SaaS startup.

### 2.2 CDP Venture Capital Funds
