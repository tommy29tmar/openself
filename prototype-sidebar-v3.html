The Interface as an Extension of Thought.

We spend our lives navigating glass rectangles. Yet, most software treats us as operators of a machine, rather than humans engaging in a dialogue.

My work is focused on reducing the friction between intent and execution. I believe in interfaces that are invisible when you are working, and beautiful when you pause to look at them.

Currently, I am shaping the design systems at Apple. Before that, I explored the digital avant-garde at Vogue.
